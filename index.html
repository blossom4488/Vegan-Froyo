Our Story

Established in early 2022, a young girl named Elfrieda Jeane, wanted more vegan options at a popular local yogurt chain. She also wanted to try an unique flavor that was not commonly found at stores or shops. She took a couple of months to test new flavors and existing flavors. Vegan Froyo was born!
